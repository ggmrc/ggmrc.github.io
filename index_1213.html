Where

The layout is located in the west basement wing of the Josephine D. Randall Junior Museum in the Corona Heights neighborhood of San Francisco.

The museum’s address is 199 Museum Way, San Francisco, California 94114-1429, USA.

2016: Due to ongoing museum current renovation, access is not possible. The museum is stated to reopen early 2017.

For more information, please visit the Randall Museum web site.
